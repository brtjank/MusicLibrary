About project

The Music Library is a project created for learning purposes by the student of Gdansk University of Technology. It is required for Internet Services Architecture laboratory classes and is based on the given example named Simple RPG

This web application provides an opportunity to store favourite songs with the possibility to add, edit, and delete them. Specific releases of songs are grouped by artist who made them.
